Thermocouple

A thermocouple is a temperature-measuring device that consists of two wires made of different metals joined at one end to form a junction. This junction, when exposed to a temperature differencetemperature difference, generates a small voltage due to the Seebeck effect, which can be measured to determine the temperature.

Hydraulic Cylinder

A hydraulic cylinder is a mechanical actuator that converts hydraulic energy into linear mechanical motion. It's a key component in hydraulic systems, using pressurized hydraulic fluid to generate force for pushing, pulling, or lifting loads. These cylinders are used in a wide variety of applications, from heavy machinery to industrial automation
